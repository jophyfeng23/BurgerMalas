Who We Are


Burger Malas was born in Penang with one simple idea — to serve affordable, tasty burgers that everyone can enjoy.

Our name “Malas” reflects our philosophy: keep things simple, no stress, no over-complication. Just good food, fast service, and a business model anyone can run.

Our Mission

To empower everyday people with a low-cost, easy-to-run business that grows together with the community.

Our Vision

To bring affordable burgers and fried chicken to every town in Malaysia.

Our Growth Model: Countryside Surrounding City

Photo from RedNote →

Instead of competing in the most expensive city spots,
Burger Malas grows from the ground up. We focus on small towns and neighborhoods first, building strong local communities before expanding into big cities. This strategy keeps costs low, risk manageable, and opportunities open for everyone. In short, we grow from the ground up, creating a foundation of trust and excellence before stepping into larger, competitive markets — a model designed for long-term success, scalability, and community impact.
